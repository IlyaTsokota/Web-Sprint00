The Infinity Stones

Name Color Powers and capabilities Image
Space Blue The stone allows its owner to exist in any surrounding space, be it space or underwater depths; move objects, yourself and other creatures to any corner of the universe; deform and rebuild space.
Time Green The stone allows you to increase the strength of the wearer's consciousness and gain access to the thoughts and dreams of other beings. Supported by the Power Stone, with its help it is possible to simultaneously access all existing minds. The stone can also release inner demons and drive them back.
Soul Orange Perhaps the most dangerous of all, this stone is intelligent and hungry that only souls can satisfy. It allows its owner to abduct, subdue, and alter the souls of the living or the dead. Adam Warlock was the only guardian of this stone for many years and felt responsible for all those souls that he himself took. Ironically, the stone is the gateway to an idyllic pocket universe.
Mind Yellow The stone allows its owner complete control over time. With its help, the past, present and future become available or visible. Perfectly wielding the stone, the wearer can use its power as a weapon, trapping enemies or even entire worlds in endless loops of time. The stone can also cause objects and creatures to physically return to adolescence or age.
Reality Red The stone has access to all the power and energy that has ever existed or will exist in the future, nourishes other stones and enhances their effects. The stone allows the wearer to duplicate almost any physical superhuman ability and become invulnerable, and therefore invincible, using only one solid strength.
Power Purple Perhaps the most powerful of all stones and the most difficult to use. The stone allows its owner to fulfill any desires, even if these desires are contrary to scientific laws. If used carelessly, the stone can lead to disaster. By touching the border of reality, it causes catastrophic damage that can only be isolated by the power of the Stone of Cosmos, the Stone of Souls and the Stone of Power.
